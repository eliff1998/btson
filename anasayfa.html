What happened at FoM20?

The Festival of Marketing went digital, delivering a week-long series of online sessions and gathering an unrivalled collection of global speakers from internationally renowned brands to address the five biggest issues faced by marketers and their brands. Festival-goers gained leading edge insight, inspiration and the learnings required to meet the challenges that lie ahead.
